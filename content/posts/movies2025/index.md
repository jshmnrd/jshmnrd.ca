# Favourite Movies
In future editions of this list I'll note if there are any changes, like if I watched a movie in the past year that made the list need to be updated then I'll say so. For this first time, here's just my list of favourite movies.

## 1. Arrival (2016)
[Letterboxd](https://letterboxd.com/film/arrival-2016/) | [IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2543164/) | [TMDB](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/329865-arrival)

![Arrival movie poster](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/1*M_5TNP9OhJ5t817t9pN9jQ@2x.jpeg)

I love this movie! Obviously, it wouldn't be #1 on my list otherwise! The funny thing about that is that I wasn't even planning on seeing it ever! I went to the theatre to see Fantastic Beasts, but they were sold out or something - I can't really remember, but we couldn't see that. So we went to see Arrival instead. Anyways, I ended up enjoying this movie so much I went back and re-watched it a few more times in theatres. 

Denis Villeneuve is now one of, if not my favourite directors - I pretty much love everything he's directed. Something about the way he composes a shot really does something for me. That combined with the music, it just - I can't really explain. It always feels like every shot in a Denis Villeneuve movie just has extreme aura. And as a bonus he's Québecois (French Canadian) so there's a bit of Canadian pride there too!

## 2. Interstellar (2014)
[Letterboxd](https://letterboxd.com/film/interstellar/) | [IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0816692/) | [TMDB](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/157336-interstellar)

![Interstellar marketing photo](https://images.bauerhosting.com/legacy/empire-tmdb/films/157336/images/xu9zaAevzQ5nnrsXN6JcahLnG4i.jpg?ar=16%3A9&fit=crop&crop=top&auto=format&w=1440&q=80)

This might seem like a very sheeple sort of take, like this is on so many people's list of favourite movies, but I really genuinely enjoy this movie so much every time I re-watch it. For me it's one of those movies I wish I could go back to the first time I watched it and experience it for the first time again. Super basic take, I know, but I don't really care.
